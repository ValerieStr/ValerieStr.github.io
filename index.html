Interests and Future Projects

Curatorial projects

My heart is with well-researched and well-designed exhibitions. Best way of learning something new and travelling in time. Revolution, Opera, Savage Beauty at Victoria and Albert, Japaneese Housing at the Barbican, Hunterian Museum - just a few of my favourites.

Filmmaking

People want entertainment! And it is an extremelly exciting process to produce it. Having worked with animation, I got really interested in production and filmmaking. I would love to work with some big animation or film company one day. My next step is to learn video editing and bring my directing and production skills to a new level.

Moral Theory

What does it mean to be a human being? What is the right thing for a person or a government to do? I have studied morality from psychological and philosophical perspecives and now doing a course on Moral Foundations of Politics on Coursera

Perfumery

I recently discovered a whole new world - a world of smells! Writing a fragrance story and creating my own perfume was one of the most fascinating experiences in my life and I am planning to do more of perfumery experiments - distilling, blending, creating!

Aesthetics

How we perceive beauty? How does art relate to power? What role has art to play in the society? Although I can see splashes of paint and brushstrokes, I find philosophy, history, and sociology of art much more attractive.

Dance Sport

Best of humanity's elegance and passion, dance sport, keeps me fit. I won the 3rd and 4th places in beginners' Quickstep and Waltz in Blackpool and I am up for more.
