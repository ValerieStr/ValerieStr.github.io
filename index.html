My heart is with well-researched and well-designed exhibitions. Best way of learning something new and travelling in time. Revolution, Opera, Savage Beauty at Victoria and Albert, Japaneese Housing at the Barbican, Hunterian Museum - just a few of my favourites.

Filmmaking

People want entertainment! And it is an extremelly exciting process to produce it. Having worked with animation, I got really interested in production and filmmaking. I would love to work with some big animation or film company one day. My next step is to learn video editing and bring my directing and production skills to a new level.

Psychology

Whether individuals or society as a whole, people are extremely fascinating. I love searching for the true reasons behind what we do and how we react to things. I am interested in behavioral & social psychology and in cognitive development and learning.
